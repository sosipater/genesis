# Vision

Genesis delivers a serious, maintainable cooking platform with two coordinated experiences:

- **Desktop-first authoring** for rich structured recipe construction.
- **Mobile-first execution** for practical, low-friction guided cooking.

The product centers on three first-class recipe sections:

1. Equipment
2. Ingredients
3. Steps

Each section is independently navigable and linked through stable references from steps.

## Product Outcomes

- Build recipes as structured entities, not text walls.
- Execute recipes with clear flow, inline timers, and linked detail access.
- Keep personal data safe across app updates and sync operations.
- Package curated bundled recipe sets without corrupting local user content.

## Design Values

- dark-mode-first, clean, practical UI
- low-friction editing and execution
- modular architecture and explicit contracts
- data safety over convenience shortcuts
- extensibility for search, collections, shopping list, and media growth
